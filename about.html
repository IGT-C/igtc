WHAT IS IGT-C?

A full-season sprint across the globe for the biggest prize in GT Racing.

The Intercontinental Gran Touring Challenge (IGT-C) is a premier global endurance racing series featuring the world's most advanced GT3 machinery. Held over six iconic circuits spanning six continents, the IGT-C tests the limits of drivers, teams, and manufacturers in a true test of speed and reliability across a full racing season.

RACE WEEKEND FORMAT

A condensed schedule for high-stakes endurance racing.

1. FREE PRACTICE (90 Menit)

Sesi latihan bebas wajib bagi semua mobil untuk menyesuaikan diri dengan trek dan kondisi.

2. QUALIFYING (30 Menit)

Sesi kualifikasi tunggal yang menentukan posisi start. Pembalap PRO dan PRO-AM wajib mencatat waktu.

3. RACE (6 JAM ENDURANCE)

Acara utama, balapan berdurasi 6 jam dengan pit stop wajib, pergantian pembalap (driver change), dan jendela *refuelling* strategis.
